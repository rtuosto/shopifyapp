# Shoptimizer User Guide

Welcome to Shoptimizer — the AI-powered conversion optimization app for Shopify stores.

Shoptimizer helps you sell more by analyzing your products, suggesting data-driven improvements, and running A/B tests automatically. You don't need any technical experience to get started. The AI does the heavy lifting — you just review its suggestions and watch your sales grow.

## Quick Start

1. **Install** Shoptimizer from the Shopify App Store.
2. **Sync** your products on the Dashboard.
3. **Generate** AI recommendations for your products.
4. **Accept** a recommendation to automatically start an A/B test.
5. **Watch** your results on the Dashboard as the system finds what works best.

Need help? Check the [FAQ](faq.md) or reach out to our support team.
